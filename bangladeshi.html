Bangladeshi

Bangladesh

Bangladeshi workers in Singapore are amongst the lowest paid foreign workers in Singapore. In Singapore, Bangladeshi workers are relatively poorly-paid and have much less money to send back to their families in Bangladesh. This is probably because they do not have the educational qualifications required for a well paid job in Singapore.

Bangladeshi workers not only have relatively lower pay compared to their counterparts from china and other countries, they also have to pay of debts which they incur from trying to get a job in the first place. These middlemen charge Bangladeshi workers a fee in exchange for a job in Singapore.
